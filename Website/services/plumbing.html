Plumbing is where most people get let down. It's small work, so it's easy for a trade to treat it as an afterthought, turn up late, make a mess and charge oddly for it.

It gets treated the same as any other job here. Booked into a slot, turned up to on time, done tidily, and priced clearly before it starts.

  • Taps, mixers, valves and stopcocks
  • Toilets, cisterns and overflows
  • Hot water cylinders and immersion heaters
  • Outside taps and garden supplies
  • Bathroom installation and replacement
  • Sinks, basins, showers and waste pipes

Leaks and water damage

A small leak is rarely just a small leak. Water finds its way into floors and ceilings, and the damage it does quietly is usually worse than the plumbing fault causing it.

If you can see water where it shouldn't be, turn the stopcock off and call. Getting to it early is almost always the difference between a simple repair and a much bigger one.

Plumbing FAQs

Do you take on small jobs?

Yes. A dripping tap gets the same care as a bathroom install. Small jobs are how people find out whether a trade is worth calling back.

Can you help with a leak urgently?

Call or text and you'll get an honest answer on how quickly we can get to you. If it's a live leak, turn off the stopcock first.

Do you fit bathrooms?

Yes, including full bathroom replacements. Get in touch to talk through what you're planning and we'll price it properly.
