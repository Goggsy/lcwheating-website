Privacy policy

What we collect, why we collect it, and what we do with it. Last updated 2026-07-13.

Who we are

LCW Heating is a Gas Safe registered heating and plumbing business operating in Catterick, Richmond and the surrounding North Yorkshire area. For any question about this policy or your data, contact us at lcwheating@gmail.com or on 07447 898088.

What we collect

We only collect the information you choose to give us through the quote form on this website:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address, if you choose to provide one
  • The service you are enquiring about and any details you write about the job

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies or analytics profiling on this website.

Why we collect it

Solely to respond to your enquiry, provide a quote, and carry out work you ask us to do. The lawful basis for this is legitimate interest and, where work proceeds, performance of a contract.

Who we share it with

We do not sell your information and we do not share it for marketing. Quote requests submitted through this website are delivered to our email inbox using Web3Forms, a form delivery service that transmits the message and does not use it for its own purposes. Email is handled by Google (Gmail).

How long we keep it

Enquiries that do not lead to work are deleted once they are clearly no longer relevant. Where work is carried out, records are kept as long as required for warranty, safety certification and tax purposes, typically six years.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you can ask us for a copy of the information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it. Email lcwheating@gmail.com and we will respond within one month.

If you are unhappy with how we handle your data, you can complain to the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.

External links

Fonts on this site are loaded from Google Fonts, which may record the IP address making the request. This site links to external websites, and we are not responsible for their privacy practices.
