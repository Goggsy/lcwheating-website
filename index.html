Questions

Frequently asked

The things people usually want to know before picking up the phone.

Are you Gas Safe registered?

Yes. LCW Heating is Gas Safe registered and fully qualified. Every gas job is tested, certified and signed off properly, and you are welcome to check the registration before any work begins.

Do you charge for quotes?

No. Quotes are free and there is no call-out charge for providing one. You get a clear price before any work starts, and it does not change unless something is agreed with you first.

Which areas do you cover?

Catterick, Catterick Garrison, Richmond, Colburn, Brompton-on-Swale, Scotton, Hipswell, Bedale, Leyburn, Northallerton, Scorton, Middleton Tyas and the surrounding North Yorkshire villages. If your postcode is not listed, just ask.

How often should a boiler be serviced?

Once a year. An annual service keeps the boiler running safely and efficiently, catches small faults before they turn into expensive ones, and is usually required to keep the manufacturer's warranty valid.

Do you provide landlord gas safety certificates?

Yes. We carry out landlord gas safety checks and issue CP12 certificates, booked around your tenants and turned around promptly.

What happens if my boiler breaks down?

Call or text and we will get to you as quickly as we can. You will be told what is wrong in plain English and given a clear price before any repair work is carried out.
